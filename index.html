    I am Mirza Salem, an Electrical and Electronics Engineer with over three years of experience in embedded systems engineering, including professional work at CIS Tech Ltd. My background spans academic research and international engineering deployments, including government-sponsored projects abroad.

    My work lies at the intersection of hardware and software, with emphasis on IoT systems, robotics, and embedded system design. I have participated in university robotics competitions and applied research projects focused on deployable engineering solutions.

    I am currently expanding my software engineering portfolio while preparing applications for graduate programs in Robotics, Embedded Systems, IoT, Renewable Energy, and Human-Robot Interaction. My research interests focus on intelligent and reliable systems that bridge the gap between laboratory research and real-world deployment.
